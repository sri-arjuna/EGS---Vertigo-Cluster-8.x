Vertigo Cluster (WIP-v0.5)
=================
Empyrion Alpha 8.x
----------------------

A scenario with several starter planets occupied by different brands.
Along the way, there are several high gravity planets around to take care of.

Say you take planet Doules or Dra as a starter planet...
All things named "AEGIS <something>" or "IDI <someting>" are there to take and use!

For any issues with vehicles or structures, please contact the specific author of it.


Starter Planets / Brands (so far)
------------------------------------
* 2 Temperate
* AEGIS, TemperateWater
* ERA, Desert - HG
* IDI, Temperate
* SEA, Desert - HG
